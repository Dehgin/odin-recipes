Buffalo Chicken Dip

Homepage

Description

  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 8 ounce softened cream cheese
  • .5 cups buffalo wing sauce
  • 1 cup grated monteray jack cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees and grease 9" pie dish
  2. In a large bowl, add chicken, cream cheese, wing sauce and 1/2 cup of each of the cheeses. Combine.
  3. Turn mixture out into prepared pie dish, smoothing top lightly with a spatula. Top with remaining cheese.
  4. Bake 30-35 minutes, or until lightly browned and bubbly. Serve immediately with tortilla chips or crackers.
